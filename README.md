# CommunityConnect AI

CommunityConnect AI is a decision-support prototype designed to help frontline workers better connect older adults with community resources, benefits, and support services.

**Live Demo:**  
https://sumaiyaripa0114-oss.github.io/communityconnect-ai/

---

## The Problem

Frontline workers often receive incomplete or overwhelming information from clients.

A single conversation may involve:

- Housing concerns
- Food insecurity
- Transportation
- Home care
- Prescription affordability
- Caregiver burnout
- Medicare and Medicaid questions

Finding the right next step can require searching multiple websites, policies, and organizations.

---

## The Solution

CommunityConnect AI organizes complex situations into structured recommendations by helping workers:

- Identify possible client needs
- Generate organized case notes
- Search policy and resource documents
- Practice empathetic conversations

The goal is to support—not replace—professional judgment.

---

## Features

- Needs Navigator
- Case Note Assistant
- Document Search
- Conversation Coach
- Mobile-friendly interface
- Demo AI responses for public demonstration

---

## Safety

This prototype:

- Does not determine eligibility
- Does not provide legal advice
- Does not replace medical professionals
- Does not replace social workers or case managers

Workers should always verify information with the appropriate agency.

---

## Future Vision

A production version would securely integrate Claude to:

- Analyze client situations
- Retrieve trusted community resources
- Generate structured summaries
- Improve frontline decision support while maintaining human oversight
