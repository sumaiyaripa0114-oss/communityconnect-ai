About CommunityConnect AI

The problem

Frontline workers often receive incomplete stories that cross Medicare, Medicaid, home care, food, housing, transportation, prescription costs, and caregiver support.

The approach

CommunityConnect AI structures the situation, surfaces connected needs, proposes questions, and points to places where current information should be verified.

Built for safety

The prototype does not determine eligibility, coverage, medical necessity, legal rights, or benefits. It is designed to support—not replace—human judgment.

Future direction

A production version could use Claude through a secure backend, organization-approved resources, role-based access, and privacy controls.
